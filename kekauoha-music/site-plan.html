Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to have a place online where current or prospective students can come and see what instruments I teach, at which levels I teach them, my teaching history/credentials, a way to contact me if they want to take/reschedule their lesson, and also a place to showcase some of my playing abilities!

Audience

My target audeince is those who are current students/parents of current students that want to check out my website or want to make changes to their lesson time. Also, those who are interested in taking lessons and want to see if I am a good fit for them and their musical journey!
