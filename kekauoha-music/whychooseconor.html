Why Choose Conor?

Always More to Learn

Conor firmly believes that you can never stop learning and that you are never too good to not need a teacher - there is always more to learn. To that end, he continues to take lessons of his own to further his playing ability, refine his technique, and to become a better teacher.

Fun and Productive

Sometimes teachers are too hard on their students, creating an environment of strict, stern discipline - without room for error. While others are too kind and lenient, leading the student to behave as if it were a playdate or hang out. However, as mentioned on the “What I Teach” page, Conor exhibits the needed patience and kindness, complemented with the necessary discipline. Doing this, Conor maintains a proper teacher/student relationship while also keeping lessons fun and productive.

Interactive Worksheets

To keep lessons fun, Conor uses outside resources that are pertinent to the current season or holiday and brings musical pieces and/or theory worksheets which reflect that! For example, during October he brings theory worksheets that have pumpkins and Fall qualities on them and pieces that are spooky and fun to remind you of Halloween!

Mulitple Platforms

Another reason to seriously consider Conor as you or your child's teacher, is he can teach through a variety of avenues. As with most people, Conor realized during the Covid pandemic how important and necessary adapting to teaching via zoom or skype is. So, if you are out of the area, feel more comfortable at home, or want the ease of just logging in, then Conor is more than happy to accommodate that need and teach you over the screen! For those who are nearby and would like Conor to come to them, he is more than capable and willing. And for those who want to come to him, that is completely acceptable as well.
